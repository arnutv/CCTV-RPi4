WIRE GAUGE & LENGTHS
─────────────────────
  Solar → MPPT     :  20 AWG, 30 cm    (any colour, polarity matters)
  MPPT → Battery   :  20 AWG, 5 cm     (RED to +, BLACK to −)
  Battery → Boost  :  20 AWG, 5 cm
  Boost → ESP32    :  22 AWG, 8 cm     (twist pair to reduce noise)

OPTIONAL ADD-ONS
─────────────────────
  Power LED        :  3 mm green, 1 kΩ resistor, across 5 V rail.
                       Visible through 3 mm hole next to lens.
  Reset button     :  Tactile switch from ESP32-CAM RST to GND,
                       through a 6 mm waterproof button in the box side.
  Battery cut-off  :  1S BMS protection module BETWEEN battery and boost.
                       Stops over-discharge below 2.5 V (saves 18650 cells).
  External antenna :  Cut the 0Ω resistor on ESP32-CAM near U.FL connector
                       (re-route from PCB antenna to U.FL → SMA pigtail).


WATERPROOFING CHECKLIST
─────────────────────
  □  IP65 box lid has rubber gasket — don't lose it
  □  PG7 cable gland on top, tightened with O-ring (silicone seal too)
  □  Lens disc glued to bezel with clear silicone (5 mm bead all round)
  □  SMA antenna connector has rubber washer + nut tight
  □  Box mounted with cable openings facing DOWN (drip away from gland)
  □  Add a 3 mm "weep hole" on the bottom for condensation, covered
     with breathable PTFE membrane sticker (Gore vent ~$0.50 each)
